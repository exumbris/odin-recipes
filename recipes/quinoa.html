Quinoa Recipe

Learn how to cook perfect, fluffy quinoa at home! Organic quinoa is a nutritious grain-like seed that is naturally free from gluten, packed full of protein and is a great alternative to rice. Perfect in salads and can even be served as porridge!

Ingredients

  • The ratio is two cups of water for every cup of quinoa

Steps

  1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly to remove any natural bitter flavour.
  2. Add rinsed quinoa and cooking liquid to a medium saucepan, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until tender and most of the liquid has been absorbed (10 to 15 minutes). You can tell that it's cooked when it is a little translucent in the centre and looks like it has a circular 'tail'.
  4. Fluff with a fork and season if desired.
  5. Add to salads or use as a side dish in place of rice.
